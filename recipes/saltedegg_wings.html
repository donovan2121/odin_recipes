Frankies' Salted Egg Wings

Crispy Chicken Wings dipped on salted-egg powder

Ingredients

Chicken Wings

  • 6 chicken wings (drum attached)
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tbsp. salt
  • Oil, for frying

Coating

  • 1/2 cup salted egg powder
  • 1/2 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. cayenne powder
  • 3 tbsp. dried kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 red chilies, thinly sliced

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the cornstarch, baking powder, and salt until combined.
  2. Add in the chicken wings and toss until evenly coated.
  3. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  4. In a food processor, add the salted egg powder, chili powder, cayenne pepper, and lime leaves.
  5. Blitz until lime leaves are in small pieces and ingredients are well combined.
  6. Add red chili and toss in powder.
  7. Heat oil in a large skillet until the temperature comes up to 375F.
  8. Fry the coated chicken wings, tapping off the excess breading before dropping in the oil.
  9. Fry the wings for 8-12 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  10. Remove the wings from the oil and set on a baking sheet with a rack or paper towels to dry.
  11. After all the chicken is fried and resting, turn off the heat.
  12. Add 2 tbsp. of hot oil to the salted egg mixture and stir together.
  13. Add the crispy chicken wings to the mixture and toss until coated evenly.
